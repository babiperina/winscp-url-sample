- You can considerer public_html folder always the base url, which is https://mylinux.langara.bc.ca/~yourstudentID

- When you get into a folder, considerer add to your base url the follow string: "/yourfoldername"

- The file that you're trying to access, will be always the last part of your URL.
Example: https://mylinux.langara.bc.ca/~yourstudentID/admin-pages/dashboard.html
